Allergen Disclaimer

While we take every effort to avoid cross-contamination, our kitchen also processes whole wheat flour, as well as tree nuts, eggs, and milk. Please consider the sensitivity of your allergy - while these allergens are not in (as much as we can possibly control) the goods we bake, they are in the kitchen we bake in.

As well, some ingredients (such as chocolate chips) we simply have to buy. Please assume that all our baked goods have been produced on equipment that processes tree nuts, coconut, eggs, soy and milk.

If you have specific concerns about an allergen, we want you to be as informed and safe as possible. Please don't hesitate to contact us with any questions.
